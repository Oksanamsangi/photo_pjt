About Me

Photography has been part of my life since childhood. I photographed my friends with anything I could get my hands on. At some point, I was gifted my first camera — and I broke it within 24 hours while experimenting. That curiosity never left me.

For a long time, I didn’t realize that the way I see people is different. I notice details others often miss. In every person, I find something unique — and through photography, I try to gently reveal it.

People often tell me that I see the frame differently. That my images feel more like moments than poses.

For several years, I was focused on reaching other goals. But one day, I had a sudden realization: photography is what truly speaks to my soul. On my birthday, I received the long-awaited camera — a turning point I’m endlessly grateful for.

Today, I’m thankful to the world for allowing me to do what I love — to see, to feel, and to create through photography.
